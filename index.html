About Me

My Name is Meleha. I have a cat named Riley. She has orange and white stripes and snores in her sleep. I've spent most of my adult life in New York City. I would describe living in NYC as having a torrid love affair. It is beautiful and raw and full of possibilites, but will stretch you to your limits and make you question your sanity. Only those who truly love New York City can handle her, and those who can, will never want to leave her.

I've climed Massada in the heat of summer, floated in the middle of the dead sea and seen the great architecture of Europe. But one thing I haven't done is climb a mountain. I long to climb mountains.
